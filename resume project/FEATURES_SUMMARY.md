## 🎉 Getting Started

1. **Start Backend**: Run `start-backend.bat` or `./start-backend.sh`
2. **Start Frontend**: Run `start-frontend.bat` or `./start-frontend.sh`
3. **Or Both**: Run `start-project.bat` or `./start-project.sh`
4. **Open Browser**: Navigate to http://localhost:5173
5. **Create Resume**: Click "Get Started" → Choose AI or Form
6. **Select Template**: Preview and choose your favorite
7. **Download**: Get your PDF resume
8. **Prepare**: Click "Prepare for Interview" for questions!
